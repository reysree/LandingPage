## Vision & Value Proposition

**"You have the expertise. We make it stick."**

Zahan (accessible at [zahan.ai](https://zahan.ai)) is an AI-powered platform designed to transform expert knowledge into interactive, live training sessions in minutes. Built by a team with backgrounds at Kahoot, Microsoft, McKinsey, and Ericsson, the project aims to solve the **"expert-to-presenter gap"**, the difficulty of effectively teaching what you know to others.

Unlike platforms like Kahoot that focus on testing existing knowledge, Zahan facilitates the actual **transfer of knowledge** through a combination of teaching, practice, and assessment in a single session. By automating the "boring" parts of session design (formatting, structure, quiz creation), it allows experts to focus on their unique insights.

<hr />

## How It Works

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Describe** | You describe the topic you want to teach, and the AI asks smart follow-up questions to refine the content. |
| **Review** | Zahan generates a complete package including slides, quizzes, exercises, and a presenter script. |
| **Practice** | Before the live session, the AI acts as a "tough audience," asking difficult questions to help you prepare. |
| **Deliver** | During the live session, participants join via QR code (no downloads required). While you share your screen, the AI sends live prompts and suggested answers to your phone to support your delivery in real-time. |

## Key Use Cases

- **New Hire Onboarding**: Quickly turning company processes into engaging sessions
- **Sales Enablement**: Training sales teams on new products with real-time feedback
- **Compliance Training**: Making mandatory training more interactive and memorable
- **Skills Workshops**: Bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application

## Real-time AI Mentorship

A unique part of Zahan's vision is the **"AI co-pilot"** concept: the AI doesn't just build the session but stays with the presenter during delivery to ensure they stay on track and can handle any question confidently. Participation is frictionless: no accounts or downloads required, so the focus remains entirely on the learning content.
